Welcome to Drive Green!

To use our application, please choose your preferences
with the drop down menus. Next, press ‘update preferences’
in order to get your results. Once you receive your results,
you may exit the application, or you may press ‘restart’ to
clear the results. Once the results are cleared, you may use
the update preferences button again to achieve new results.

Your result vehicle names are shortened for formatting when
applicable. Please click on the shortened name and a dialog 
box will show you the whole name.
